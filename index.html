Welcome to my personal website.

I am Kwabla, a professional teacher and researcher with extensive training in mathematics and economics. I earned a BA in Mathematics and Economics from the University of Ghana (2013), an MSc in Mathematics from the University of Cape Coast (2016), a PGDE from the University of Education Winneba (2020), and an MA in Economics from York University (2024).

I am currently pursuing a PhD in Economics, focusing on advanced quantitative and analytical methods. From 2017 to 2023, I taught at the professional level, combining my teaching experience with data-driven research. My technical skills include Python, SQL, R, Excel, and MATLAB, which I use for statistical modeling, economic analysis, and problem-solving.

Beyond academics and work, I love programming and soccer, which keep me creative, active, and engaged. I am passionate about leveraging mathematics and technology to solve real-world problems and contribute to research and education.
